> **The average unpaid caregiver spends $7,200/year out-of-pocket** and receives zero training for complex medical tasks they perform daily. Caregiver burnout is a public health crisis hiding in plain sight — 63% higher mortality rate than non-caregivers.

## The Solution

Care coordination, respite care finder, guided training for medical tasks, burnout early warning, benefits navigation.

## Agents

**CareCoordinator** — Medication schedules, appointments, insurance claims, care transitions
**RespiteFinder** — Local respite care resources matched to your situation
**TrainingGuide** — Step-by-step guides for specific care tasks (wound care, feeding tubes, dementia communication)
**BurnoutMonitor** — Self-reported mood/sleep/stress tracking with early warning alerts
**BenefitsNavigator** — FMLA, VA caregiver programs, Medicaid waivers, tax deductions

## Safety

If caregiver expresses suicidal thoughts or severe distress, MANDATORY 988 handoff.

## Data Sources

AARP/NAC caregiver surveys, CMS assessment tools, state Medicaid waivers, VA Caregiver Support, Area Agency on Aging
